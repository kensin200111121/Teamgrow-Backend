Thank you for choosing to use our Sleep Mode for your crmgrow account. Below are details about what happens during Sleep Mode and any third-party apps you may have added, like the Wavv Dialer.
Important Details
  • The Sleep Mode option will bill each month to maintain the Sleep Mode status of your crmgrow account.
  • If billing fails, we will contact you by email asking you to update your credit card information.
    • You will receive information on how to make that update on that email.
  • When you are ready to “wake up” your crmgrow account, and remove Sleep Mode;
    • Log into your account with crmgrow at https://app.crmgrow.com/
    • Click on your name at the top right of the dashboard
    • Click on My Profile
    • Click on the Subscription tab
    • Choose your plan and confirm it
  • If you have subscribed to any third-party apps, such as the Wavv Dialer click-to-call, single-line dialer, or multi-line dialer, they will continue to be billed.
    • Click here >>> Dialer Account Information and Billing to learn how to cancel the Wavv Dialer while you are in sleep mode.
    • If you cancel a third-party service while your crmgrow account is in sleep mode, you must add it back once you are active again.
    • If you have previously had the Wave Dialer with crmgrow, you will not be eligible to receive the two-week trial again.
